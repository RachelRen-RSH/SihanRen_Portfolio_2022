About me

       Hello, this is Sihan. Nice to meet you here. I'm a senior undergraduate majoring in Cartography & GIS and Computer Science at UW-Madison.
       I love the combination of technologies and creativity, which is why I call myself an art programmer. It has been a fantastic journey since I made my first cartography "masterpiece" in high school. Six years later, I became a professional cartographer. Working at Robinson's Map Library, Wisconsin State Cartographer's Office and Sea Grant Institute were the most remarkable experiences of my college life.
       I am fueled by my passion for discovering social issues and turning them into visual stories for the public. As an international student, I am interested in comparing city design patterns in different countries. After graduating, I plan to pursue a master's degree in geographic spatial analysis or urban planning.
